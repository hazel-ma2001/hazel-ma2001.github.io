Hi! I am a Master's student at Tsinghua University. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Engineering Mechanics at Tsinghua and pursued the Diplôme d'Ingénieur (expected June 26) at CentraleSupélec in Paris. My footprints span China (Tsinghua), France (CentraleSupélec), the UK (Oxford), and the US (Berkeley & MIT). I feel incredibly grateful and honored to have had the opportunity to work with the most inspiring people at these amazing places.

I am interested in human-centered AI/robotics across layers (multi-modal cognitive AI, physical human-robot interaction, etc.). I want to make AI/robotic systems understand human intent and provide meaningful assistance and empathetic support, both in daily life and in decision-making tasks.

I love nature and music as much as I love science. I enjoy writing blogs and taking photos to document the world around me.
